No Bean Turkey and Sweet Potato Chili

--- Time ---
0hr 30min

--- Servings ---
2

--- Category ---
dinner, lunch, party

--- Instructions ---
1. Saute 1 lb ground turkey in instant pot. Season with salt and cumin to taste.
2. When meat is cooked, add 1/2 cup chopped onion and 3 crushed garlic cloves. Cook for 3 more minutes.
3. Add can of mild tomatoes with green chilies, 1 sweet potato chopped, 1 can tomato sauce, 3/4 cups water, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/4 tsp chili powder, 1/4 tsp paprika, salt, and 1 bay leaf.
4. Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes. Natural release.
5. Remove bay leaf and serve.
